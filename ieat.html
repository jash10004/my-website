Mission

Provide excellent instruction, conduct relevant research and foster community engagement that produce highly competent graduates necessary for the development of the country.

Vision

A globally-engaged higher education institution of agriculture and allied disciplines

Goals

a. Produce competent professionals with current knowledge and skills, ready to flourish in a dynamic and globally competitive environment.
b. Pursue innovative research, engaging extension, production, and capacity development activities that promote digital literacy and social responsibility.
c. Foster a strong collaboration with industry and government institutions to empower students to attain their full potential and enhance faculty teaching and research capabilities.
d. Ensure the delivery of high-quality education across the various fields of study offered by the institute.
