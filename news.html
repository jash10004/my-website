BASC Hosts SCUFAR 3 February 2025 Meeting

The Bulacan Agricultural State College (BASC) hosted the State Colleges and Universities Faculty Association Region III (SCUFAR 3) Regular Monthly Meeting held February 4 – 5, 2025 at the BASC Function Hall.

This event brought together faculty regents, trustees, and sports and cultural coordinators from 12 state universities and colleges (SUCs) in the region.

BASC Achieves Full Compliance with FOI Requirements

Bulacan Agricultural State College (BASC) is proud to announce its full compliance with the enhanced Freedom of Information (FOI) requirements set by the Presidential Communications Office (PCO), a crucial criterion for the grant of the Performance-Based Bonus (PBB) for Fiscal Year 2023.

BASC Ranks 163rd in UniRanks 2024: Strengthening Excellence in Higher Education

The Bulacan Agricultural State College (BASC) continues to make its mark in the country’s academic landscape, ranking 163rd in the Philippines in the recently released UniRanks 2024. This recognition underscores BASC’s commitment to providing quality education and fostering a culture of academic excellence and institutional growth.

BASC Ranks 6th Top Performing School in LET Elementary Category for 2024

Bulacan Agricultural State College (BASC) proudly secured the 6th spot among the top performing schools with 100 or more takers in the elementary category of the March 2024 Licensure Examination for Teachers (LET). This outstanding achievement reflects the dedication and academic excellence of our graduates, faculty, and staff

BASC Achieves Remarkable Standing in UI GreenMetric World University Rankings 2024

Bulacan Agricultural State College (BASC) proudly announces its outstanding performance in the 2024 UI GreenMetric World University Rankings. The results, released on December 12, 2024, during a special ceremony in Brazil, position BASC as 13th in the Philippines, 276th in Asia, and 506th globally. This remarkable achievement underscores BASC’s commitment to sustainability and environmental stewardship.
