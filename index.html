Our Story

Wear the dream. Live the story.

What started as a simple dream between two souls in love became something bigger than we ever imagined. Fueled by our shared passion for fashion and creativity, we built this brand from the ground up—each design reflecting not just style, but the story of our journey together.

From late-night sketches to handpicking fabrics, every piece we create is made with heart, dedication, and the dream that inspired it all. This isn’t just a clothing brand; it’s a love story woven into every thread. And now, we’re sharing it with you.
